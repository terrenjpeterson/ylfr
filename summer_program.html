Summer Programs

When the school year breaks, we offer structured summer activities Monday through Thursday from 9:00 AM â€“ 3:00 PM. Hands-on math, economics, science, and social studies are also offered in the afternoons for children in second grade through high school.


Summer Interns work together with our Learning Center staff to provide innovative and engaging learning experiences for our students. Additionally, we register and sponsor children to attend a summer camp designed for youth from urban communities. We also sponsor an incentive based mystery trip at the end of the summer program. Students must maintain an average of 1.5(out of a possible 2) behavioral points for the year to qualify for this trip.
